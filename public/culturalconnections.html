Cultural Description

Top Two Cultural Shocks in Vancouver:

  • Shock 1: The cultural diversity in Vancouver is impressive, with a mix of many cultures that isn't as prevalent in Peru.
  • Shock 2: The more relaxed lifestyle and the emphasis on work-life balance in Canada differ from the work culture in Peru.

Top Two Things I Miss Most from Peru:

  • Miss 1: Peruvian food, especially dishes like ceviche and criollo cuisine that are hard to find here.
  • Miss 2: The cultural festivities and celebrations, such as Semana Santa and the Beer Festival.

Amazing New Things I Love About Canada:

  • Love 1: The natural beauty of Canada, from mountains to lakes, is stunning and different from anywhere in Peru.
  • Love 2: The friendliness and openness of people towards immigrants and cultural diversity.
